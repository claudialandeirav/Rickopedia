About

Welcome to Rick and Morty Universe!

Rick, a brilliant scientist, and Morty, his impressionable grandson, live dangerous adventures on other planets and parallel dimensions.

"Rick and Morty" is an animated adult series that follows the adventures of Rick Sanchez, a genius and alcoholic scientist, and his grandson Morty Smith. Together, they explore alternate dimensions, face surreal situations, and tackle scientific challenges. The series blends science fiction with black and satirical humor, addressing profound themes while breaking the fourth wall and providing social commentary. The relationship between Rick and his family, along with improvisation in the writing, are key aspects that contribute to the show's appeal.
