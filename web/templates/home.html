What is Rick and Morty?

Rick and Morty is an American animated science fiction sitcom created by Justin Roiland and Dan Harmon. The series premiered on Adult Swim, a late-night programming block on Cartoon Network, in December 2013. The show follows the misadventures of an eccentric and alcoholic scientist named Rick Sanchez and his good-hearted but easily influenced grandson, Morty Smith. Together, they embark on various interdimensional and intergalactic adventures, encountering bizarre creatures, visiting alternate realities, and exploring complex themes such as existentialism, family dynamics, and the nature of reality. Rick and Morty is known for its dark humor, intricate storytelling, and inventive science fiction concepts, and has gained a dedicated fanbase over the years.

This is Rick

Rick is a brilliant yet deeply flawed and cynical scientist in "Rick and Morty." He's known for his reckless behavior, heavy drinking, and disdain for authority. Despite his flaws, he's incredibly intelligent and inventive, often creating mind-bending inventions. Rick's complex personality includes a mix of nihilism, independence, and occasional moments of genuine care, especially toward his grandson Morty.

This is Morty

Morty is a good-hearted teenager reluctantly dragged into interdimensional adventures with his eccentric grandfather, Rick. He's characterized by his nervousness, insecurity, and moral compass, but grows more confident and capable over time.
